Who we are

Introducing Café Tomes, spearheaded by Shabaz Singh, dedicated to bolstering food security and combating poverty in the vibrant city of Toronto, Ontario.With our focal points nestled in Brampton and Mississauga , we are committed to providing equal opportunities for all to access wholesome, culturally diverse, and nourishing meals. Our mission extends beyond mere sustenance; we strive to ignite economic revitalization in the communities we serve.

Mission

We’re on a mission to end hunger coast to coast. With innovative grocery delivery and a relentless hunger helpline, we’re transforming lives and creating a community where no one sleeps on an empty stomach.
